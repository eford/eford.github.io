## Interests
- Extreme Precision Radial Velocities
- Stellar Variability Mitigation
- Line-by-line Spectral Analysis
- NEID

Kanishk develops stellar variability mitigation techniques for extreme precision radial velocity surveys, exploring the potential of line-by-line measurements using NEID observations during solar eclipses.
