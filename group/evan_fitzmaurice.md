## Interests
- Radial Velocity Characterization
- Evaluating Stellar Variability Mitigation Algorithms.
- Brown Dwarfs
- Gaia Astrometry
- HPF & NEID

Evan characterizes exoplanets and brown dwarfs using radial velocity observations with HPF and NEID, combined with astrometry from Gaia.  Evan is leading a NEID program to test stellar variability mitigration strategies developed for the Sun on other stars.

## Selected Papers
- Fitzmaurice, E. et al. (2024). "Astrometry and Precise Radial Velocities Yield a Complete Orbital Solution for the Nearby Eccentric Brown Dwarf LHS 1610 b."
- Fitzmaurice, E. et al. (2022). MNRAS, 512, 1436. "A Hot Mass-density Companion to TIC 320687387, Spectroscopic Confirmation of a Brown Dwarf around an Old M Dwarf."
- Gilbertson, C. et al. (2024). "Data-Driven Modeling of Telluric Features and Stellar Variability with StellarSpectraObservationFitting.jl."
